Why We Started Lezzet

Lezzet (Turkish for "flavor") was born from a simple frustration: you can't find fresh Turkish coffee in America.

Walk into any grocery store and the Turkish coffee on the shelf was roasted months ago in Turkey. It sat in customs. It sat in a warehouse. By the time you open it, the flavor is gone.

We decided to change that.

What Makes Lezzet Different

Lezzet is specialty-grade Turkish coffee, freshly roasted in small batches right here in the USA. We source Ethiopian Arabica for bright, fruity notes and Brazilian Arabica for smooth, chocolatey body. Ground to traditional powder-fine consistency. Shipped fresh — within days of roasting, not months.

More Than Coffee

Turkish coffee isn't just a drink. It's a 500-year-old ritual. The slow brew on low heat. The foam rising in the cezve. The first sip from a tiny cup. The fortune hidden in the grounds.

We're not reinventing that tradition — we're making it accessible with the freshest, highest-quality coffee possible.
