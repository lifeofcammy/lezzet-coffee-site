# CLAUDE.md — Lezzet Turkish Coffee

> Turkish coffee brand launch project — planning, branding, and go-to-market.
> **Last updated: March 11, 2026**

## Brand

- **Name:** Lezzet (Turkish for "flavor/taste")
- **Full:** Lezzet Specialty Grade Turkish Coffee
- **Tagline:** Taste the Ritual
- **Logo:** Cezve + Ottoman tulip + teal arch on cream background
- **Domain:** lezzetcoffee.com
- **Shopify store:** lezzetcoffee.com (Horizon theme)

## Product

- **Hero SKU:** 12oz Classic — Ethiopia & Brazil Arabica blend, specialty grade, finely ground
- **Roaster:** Friend's business, $10.50/unit at 200+ qty, no minimum, 2-day lead time
- **Pricing:** $21.99 Amazon / $19.99 Shopify
- **Future SKUs:** Cardamom ($23.99), Mastic, Discovery Sampler

## Key Numbers

- COGS: $10.50/unit (12oz at 200+ units)
- Amazon net per unit: ~$3.53 (after fees + PPC)
- Shopify net per unit: ~$5.49 (after shipping + processing)
- Break-even: 58 units/month
- Startup budget: ~$1,800-$3,800
